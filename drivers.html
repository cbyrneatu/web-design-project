Much like the teams in Formula 1, drivers also have their own championship, the drivers championship.

Points are awarded for the drivers who finish in the top 10, and whoever has the most points at the end of the year will win the drivers championship.

If a driver finishes within the top 10, and sets a fastest lap for the race, they will be awarded an additional point.
