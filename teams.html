Teams

There are 10 teams on the current Formula 1 grid, each team having two drivers. When a driver wins a race, both the team and a driver get a certain amount of points, depending on their finishing position in the race. If the driver finishes below 10th place, neither the team, or the driver, get the points.

Teams are allowed to source Power Units from other teams, they do not have to make the entire car themselves. However, there is strict rules around blatantly copying other car designs, which can result in severe penalties for the team.

It's the goal of every team in Formula 1 to get the most amount of points possible, this is called the Constructors Championship.
